Bircher Müsli

This delicious "Bircher Müsli" prepares you perfectly for the day. As breakfast, it offers you all the important nutrients to enable a sporty start. Of course, it is also perfect as a small snack to satisfy hunger.

The preparation is super simple and is especially suitable for preparing the muesli the day before. The dish is also known as "Overnight Oats".

  • 40g oats
  • 20g seeds
  • 10g peanut butter
  • 200ml almond milk
  • 10g cashews
  1. prepare a glas which can be closed
  2. Layer the ingredients according to the following order
    1. oats
    2. seeds
    3. cashews
  3. fill in the almond milk
  4. top with the peanut butter
